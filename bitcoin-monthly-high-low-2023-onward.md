## How to verify or pull your own series

1. **Exchange API (most precise):**
   - Binance: `GET https://api.binance.com/api/v3/klines?symbol=BTCUSDT&interval=1M&limit=60`
   - Coinbase: `GET https://api.exchange.coinbase.com/products/BTC-USD/candles?granularity=86400` (daily, then aggregate to monthly)
2. **Index providers:** CoinGecko `/coins/bitcoin/market_chart/range`, CoinMarketCap historical.
3. **Charting:** TradingView monthly candles (`BTCUSD` or `BTCUSDT`) — hover candle for OHLC.

Standardize on **one venue or one index** before comparing months — that prevents small but real mismatches between sources.
