**Purpose:** Curated index of **primary** sources for understanding how Strategy (formerly MicroStrategy) explains its **business model**, **Bitcoin treasury strategy**, **capital structure**, and **software business**.

**Disclaimer:** Links and filing paths change over time. Always use the latest **10-K**, **10-Q**, and **8-K** on [SEC EDGAR](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/browse/?CIK=1050446). This list is not investment advice.

## 1. How the company frames strategy (official summary)

Strategy’s **Investor Relations** page states, in summary:

- It is the **world’s first and largest Bitcoin Treasury Company** (public, Nasdaq: MSTR).
- **Bitcoin** is the **primary treasury reserve asset**; the company accumulates BTC using **equity and debt financings** and **operating cash flows** where applicable.
- The treasury approach is meant to give investors **different degrees of economic exposure to Bitcoin** through **multiple securities** (common, convertibles, preferred / “digital credit” style instruments).
- In parallel, it sells **AI-powered enterprise analytics software** under the **Intelligence Everywhere** vision and ties **operational excellence**, **BTC reserve**, and **innovation** together in its positioning.

**Source:** [Strategy — Investor Relations](https://www.strategy.com/investor-relations)

## 2. Must-read regulatory documents (strategy in legal / financial form)

| Document | Why it matters for strategy |
|----------|------------------------------|
| **Annual Report on Form 10-K** | **Item 1. Business** — products, markets, competition, BTC treasury narrative. **Item 1A. Risk Factors** — concentration, volatility, dilution, regulation, cyber. **Item 7. MD&A** — management’s explanation of results and liquidity. **Item 7A.** — market risk (including digital assets). |
| **Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q** | Updated MD&A, risk factors, and digital-asset footnotes each quarter. |
| **Current Reports on Form 8-K** | Material events: BTC purchases, financings, leadership, significant agreements. Exhibits often include **investor decks** (e.g. `EX-99.1`). |

**Portal:** [SEC EDGAR — Strategy / MicroStrategy (CIK 1050446)](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/browse/?CIK=1050446)

**Tip:** Open the latest **10-K** PDF or HTML and search for **“bitcoin”**, **“digital assets”**, **“ATM”**, **“convertible”**, **“preferred”**.
